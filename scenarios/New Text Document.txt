Appointment Cancellation

Appointment.status = “Cancelled.”
Bill generation skipped or linked Bill.status = “Cancelled.”
All related AppointmentService entries remain for audit.


Doctor Leaving

Keep doctor record but mark active = false.
FK in Appointment and MedicalRecord preserved to maintain history.
Department.head_doctor_id updates to new head.





Partial Payments

Multiple Payment entries per Bill, allowing sum(payment.amount) < bill.total_amount.
Bill.status updates dynamically (Open | Partially Paid | Paid).




Service Price Changes

Each price change creates a new Service record or a new entry in a ServicePriceHistory table (if added).
AppointmentService.unit_price captures snapshot at booking time so bills remain accurate historically.
