 Entities
Patient
Stores all demographic and contact details.
Doctor
Contains professional details and specialization.
Department
Defines hospital divisions (Cardiology, Pediatrics, etc.).
Appointment
Links patient and doctor; stores visit details.
Service
Represents any test, treatment, or procedure available.
AppointmentService (bridge entity)
Resolves many‑to‑many relationship between Appointment and Service; includes Quantity and UnitPrice.
MedicalRecord
Stores diagnosis, treatment notes, and prescriptions.
Bill
Represents the financial document for an appointment.
Payment
Stores one or more payments against a bill (supports partial payments).

Relationships (raw form)
Patient books Appointment → 1‑to‑many
Doctor attends Appointment → 1‑to‑many
Department has Doctors → 1‑to‑many
Doctor heads Department → optional 1‑to‑1
Appointment includes Service → many‑to‑many via AppointmentService
Appointment generates MedicalRecord → 1‑to‑1 (optional)
Appointment produces Bill → 1‑to‑1
Bill receives Payment → 1‑to‑many
Service appears in many Appointments → many‑to‑many
